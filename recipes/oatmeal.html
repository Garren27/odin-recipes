Oatmeal

Description

Oatmeal is a nutritious and delicious food that can be enjoyed at any time and customized to your liking

The base may be plain, but the possiblities are endless with the combinations of toppings

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup oatmeal
  • Enough water to cover
  • (Optional) Maple Syrup

Instructions

  1. Add oatmeal to bowl
  2. Add water until oatmeal is just covered
  3. Microwave on high for 90 seconds
  4. Add toppings
  5. Let cool for 2 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
